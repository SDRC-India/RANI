About Us

The Reduction in Anemia through Normative Innovations (RANI) is a BMGF funded research project currently under implementation in 130 villages of Athmalik & Kishorenagar blocks of Angul district of Odisha. RANI is a social norms-based intervention aiming to reduce anemia among women of reproductive age (WRA) by promoting the consumption of iron folic acid (IFA) along with nutritious food.

By design, RANI is a cluster randomized control trial led by the George Washington University in partnership with IPE Global and DCOR Consulting. RANI collaborates with the Odisha Livelihood Mission capitalising on the strength of the SHGs promoted by them in Angul.

This MIS monitors the T4 approach which directs the field level intervention activities described as below.
